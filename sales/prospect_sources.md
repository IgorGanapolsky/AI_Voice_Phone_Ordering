# Prospect Sources

Use these sources to build a voice-agent prospect list:

- Google Business restaurants in Miami with visible phone-first ordering
- Toast/Square/Clover order pages where phone number is prominent
- Restaurants with catering, reservations, or after-hours voicemail language
- Sandwich shops, cafes, bakeries, Cuban restaurants, and QSR-style operators with rush-hour demand
- Public approved-caterer lists where phone/email are explicitly published

Best first-fit signals:

- Phone number is more prominent than online ordering
- Reviews mention hard-to-reach staff, long waits, or busy rush periods
- Menu is simple enough for scripted order capture
- Owner/operator is local and likely reachable
- Restaurant already accepts pickup, reservations, or catering inquiries by phone

Avoid for first batch:

- Large enterprise chains with centralized procurement
- Complex fine-dining menus with heavy allergy/customization risk
- Restaurants that require payment before every order unless POS integration is already approved
- Leads already contacted by another active campaign in the last 24 hours
